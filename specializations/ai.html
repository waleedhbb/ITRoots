Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is the study and creation of intelligent machines capable of mimicking human behaviors, such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making. AI technologies aim to develop systems that can analyze large amounts of data, recognize patterns, and make predictions, without the need for explicit programming for every task.

AI is revolutionizing industries by enabling automation and decision-making across a wide range of domains. It powers applications like virtual assistants, autonomous vehicles, and predictive analytics. AI is enhancing productivity in sectors such as healthcare (through diagnostic tools), finance (by detecting fraud), and manufacturing (via automation).
